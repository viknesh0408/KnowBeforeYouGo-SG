Important Laws & Regulations

  • 🚭 Smoking is prohibited in public areas — only smoke in designated zones.
  • 🚫 Drug trafficking and possession carry the death penalty.
  • 💰 Littering, vandalism, and jaywalking are punishable by fines.
  • 🧾 Chewing gum sales and imports are restricted.
  • 📸 Avoid photographing government or restricted areas.

Do’s & Don’ts in Singapore

Do’s
  • Be punctual and respectful.
  • Use public transport responsibly.
  • Keep the environment clean.
  • Stand left on escalators.
  • Respect cultural diversity.
Don’ts
  • Don’t litter or spit.
  • Don’t eat or drink in public transport.
  • Don’t bring illegal chewing gum.
  • Don’t make offensive comments on religion or race.

Travel Tips for Visitors

Getting Around

Use MRT and buses for convenience. Grab an EZ-Link or bank card for travel.

Weather

Hot and humid all year (~30°C). Carry water and an umbrella for rain.

Connectivity

Buy prepaid SIM cards from Singtel or StarHub. Free Wi-Fi: Wireless@SG.

Contact & Helplines

📞 Emergency Numbers
  • Police: 999
  • Ambulance & Fire: 995
  • Tourist Hotline: 1800-736-2000
