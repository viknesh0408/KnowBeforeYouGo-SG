After Applying Any Work Pass

  • Work Permit applications take around 1 working day to get approved.
  • S-Pass and other work pass applications take between 5 days and 3 weeks.
  • You can check your application status on this official MOM website.
  • If status shows Pending, wait or ensure your employer submits required documents.
  • If status shows Valid, your IPA (In-Principle Approval) is ready.
  • Ask your employer for the IPA stating your occupation, company, and salary.
  • Cross-check the IPA for differences from what you were promised.
  • Working in a different job or for less salary than your IPA states is a crime ‚Äî you can be deported.
  • Contact MOM (Ministry of Manpower) if unsure about your company.
  • Buy the ACRA company details file for SGD 5.50 if you want to verify a company.
  • If you want to cancel the process, call MOM to inform them.

After Arriving in Singapore

  • Your employer must issue your work pass before you start work.
  • You cannot start work before your pass is issued.
  • After issuance, go for thumbprint registration.
  • Your Work Pass card will arrive by post within 5 working days.
