Find the right pass

Browse core Singapore passes — work, study and dependent schemes. Click a card for details and application notes.

Employment Pass (EP)

For professionals, managers, executives
Work
Eligibility: Job offer in Singapore with qualifying salary and credentials.
Duration: Up to 2 years, renewable.
Main documents: Passport, employment contract, degree/certificates.

S Pass

For mid-skilled foreign workers
Work
Eligibility: Minimum qualifying salary, quota dependent on employer.
Duration: Up to 2 years, renewable.
Main documents: Employer application, passport, payslips, education certificates.

Work Permit (WP)

For semi-skilled or unskilled workers (e.g., construction, marine)
Work
Eligibility: Depends on nationality and sector; employer must apply.
Duration: Shorter terms, sector-dependent.
Notes: Quotas, levies and standard employment rules apply.

EntrePass

For entrepreneurs/startup founders
Startup
Eligibility: New business with innovation potential or meeting qualifying criteria.
Duration: Typically 1–2 years, renewable if business milestones met.
Docs: Business plan, investor letters, company incorporation documents.

Personalised Employment Pass (PEP)

For high-earning professionals (no employer tie)
Premium
Eligibility: High fixed salary or prior EP with high earnings.
Duration: Up to 3 years, not tied to employer.
Note: Allows job-switching without new pass application.

Dependant's Pass (DP)

For spouses and children of qualifying pass holders
Family
Eligibility: Holder of sufficient qualifying pass (e.g., EP).
Duration: Matches main pass validity.
Note: DP holders may need work permission to take up employment.

Long-Term Visit Pass (LTVP)

For extended family members or accompanying spouses (conditional)
Family
Eligibility: Depends on relationship and sponsor's status.
Duration: Varies; often renewable.
Note: Employment eligibility may be restricted.

Student Pass

For full-time students at approved institutions
Study
Eligibility: Admission offer from a registered institution.
Duration: Tied to course duration.
Docs: Admission letter, proof of funds, passport.

Quick tips

  • Always check eligibility and quota rules before applying.
  • Applications are typically submitted by employers or sponsors.
  • Keep certified copies of education and employment records ready.
  • Processing times and fees vary by pass type.

When to contact MOM

  • For clarifications on eligibility or appeals.
  • To report issues with pass issuance or cancellation.
